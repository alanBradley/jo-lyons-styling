Primary for the main action. Ghost for secondary. Ghost-light only on dark backgrounds.

07 · Voice

How Jo sounds.

First person. Short sentences. Understated. Never shouty.
Do
  • Write in the first person. "I work with brides."
  • Short, confident sentences. Let them land.
  • Lead with what you do for the reader, not about yourself.
  • Use the em-italic sparingly, for the one word that carries feeling.
Don't
  • Don't use exclamation marks.
  • Don't pile on adjectives. One well-chosen word beats three.
  • Don't sound like a marketing page. Sound like a person.
  • Don't hype. The craft speaks for itself.
